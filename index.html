My Groovy Art

Let me introduce myself. I am an artist who specializes in mind-altering psychedelic painting...which I think are really "groovy." So that's why I call my online store "Groovy Art," obviously! So, if you want a little insanity for your den, man-cave, or music venue, look no further than Groovy Art! I have a series of acrylic paintings that I have done over the last few years or so. I hope you like them and find them...GROOVY! Peace out."
